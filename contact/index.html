Contact Us

Get in touch with our team. We're here to help!

Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, suggestions, or just want to say hello, there are several ways to reach us. Our team reads every message and responds within 24-48 hours during business days.

Email Addresses

To help us route your inquiry efficiently, please use the appropriate email address:

General Inquiries

hello@moltbook-guide.com

Questions about the website, general information requests, or just want to say hi? This is the right address. We love hearing from readers and welcome any non-technical questions or comments.

Content Suggestions

content@moltbook-guide.com

Have an idea for a tutorial? Spot an error in our documentation? Want to contribute an article? Our content team reviews all suggestions and uses them to improve our resources.

Technical Support

support@moltbook-guide.com

Running into issues with our tutorials or need help troubleshooting a problem? Our technical support team is here to assist. Please describe the issue in detail, including any error messages and steps you've already tried.

Business Inquiries

business@moltbook-guide.com

For partnership opportunities, sponsorship inquiries, or enterprise requests, please contact our business development team. We review all business proposals and respond to relevant inquiries within 72 hours.

Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible:

  • General inquiries: 24-48 hours
  • Content suggestions: 48-72 hours
  • Technical support: 24-48 hours (longer for complex issues)
  • Business inquiries: 72 hours

What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you more effectively, please include:

  • Your name and preferred contact email
  • The nature of your inquiry (general, content, support, business)
  • Relevant details (URLs, error messages, specific questions)
  • What you've already tried (for support requests)
  • Your preferred outcome or what you're hoping to accomplish

Office Hours

Our team operates primarily during business hours in the following time zones:

  • North America: 9 AM - 6 PM EST
  • Europe: 9 AM - 6 PM CET
  • Asia Pacific: 9 AM - 6 PM JST

While we strive to maintain these hours, team members in different regions may respond outside these windows, ensuring 24-hour coverage for urgent matters.

Location

Moltbook Guide is a distributed team with members around the world. We work remotely and collaborate online, allowing us to serve our global audience effectively while maintaining diverse perspectives and expertise.

Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve! After we respond to your inquiry, you may receive a brief survey. We appreciate honest feedback about your experience and use it to enhance our support processes.

Press Inquiries

Members of the press seeking information about Moltbook Guide, our mission, or our team should contact press@moltbook-guide.com. We can provide high-resolution images, interview opportunities, and background information for stories about collaborative AI and educational technology.

Thank You

We appreciate you taking the time to reach out. Every message helps us improve and better serve the Moltbook community. Whether you're a longtime reader or just discovering our resources, we're grateful for your interest and support.

We look forward to hearing from you and helping you on your Moltbook journey!
